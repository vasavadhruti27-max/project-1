1. Agency's Official Website:

    • Look for a "Contact," "Get in Touch," or "Work With Us" page.
    • Check the website's footer for a quick email address or phone number.
    • Sometimes, an agency's "About" or "Team" page will list key people with their email addresses.

  2. Professional Directories & Review Sites:

    • Websites like Clutch.co, DesignRush, Upwork, and Sortlist list thousands of agencies and often include their website, phone number, and a contact form.
    • Searching on Google Maps for "design agency [City Name]" will usually bring up local results with a phone number and address.

  3. Social Media:

    • Check their LinkedIn page, as they often list key contact people there.
    • Their Instagram or Facebook profile often includes an email address or a "Contact" button.
